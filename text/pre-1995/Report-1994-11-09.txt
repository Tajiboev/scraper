               project graphic patterns of red light.  Recall #Z-049-5.
CODE           Model Delta 200.
MANUFACTURER   Laserworks, Santa Ana, California.
RECALLED BY    Manufacturer.  FDA approved the firm's corrective action
               plan October 7, 1994. Firm-initiated field correction
               ongoing.
DISTRIBUTION   Domestic and foreign.
QUANTITY       4 units.
REASON         Noncompliance with federal laser product performance
               standard in that product was improperly classified as Class
               IIIa; product was reported as a Class IIIa but could be used
               with a Class IIIb or Class IV laser for which a variance is
               required; no variance had been issued.  Safety interlocks
               would be required if protective housing is required to be
               removed during performance of any operational or maintenance
               procedures.  Product lacked some performance features
               required of a Class IIIb laser product: Key switch, remote
               interlock connector; product lacked labeling requirements;
               and user instructions lacked adequate instructions and
               warnings for proper assembly and safe use of the product.
